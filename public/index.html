Spell Project

by Matthew Walker and Avery Mao

A website for the administration of the School of Magic to register students, masters, and their associated spells. Students are assigned to one master during a single year. Each student and master may know up to 50 spells.

Spells
Page to add, remove, or edit existing spells.

Classes
Page to add, remove, or edit existing classes.

Masters
Page to add, remove, or edit existing masters.

Students
Page to add, remove, or edit existing Students.

View Masters' Spells
